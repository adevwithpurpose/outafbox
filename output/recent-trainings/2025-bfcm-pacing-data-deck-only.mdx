Recent Trainings

Every year, the hour-by-hour volume of demand across BFCM weekend follows a predictable curve:

A spike on Thursday evening

A huge jump on Black Friday starting around 7 am EST and peaking around 9-10 am EST

Smaller (but still significant) peaks on Saturday around 9 am EST and Sunday around 1 pm EST

A massive peak around 8 pm EST on Cyber Monday
