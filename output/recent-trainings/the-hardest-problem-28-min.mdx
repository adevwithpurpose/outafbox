The Hardest Problem (28 min)

Recent Trainings

In this video, we're tackling the tough challenge of declining revenue in ecommerce. Whether you're a store owner, marketer, or finance pro, if your revenue has dipped in the past two years, this lesson is for you.

This episode is brought to you by:

Finaloop:

https://www.finaloop.com/

Wayflyer:

https://www.wayflyer.com/

Join Taylor Holiday, CEO of Common Thread Collective, with our partners at Wayflyer and Finaloop. Together, we'll navigate solutions distilled from eight episodes of learning. Reflect on the familiar story depicted in the graphs. Many ecommerce brands, including my own, faced rapid growth followed by a decline in revenue. The key lies in aligning marketing and finance efforts.

Step one: "squeezing the sponge." We'll recalibrate margins and expenses to ensure survival.

Step two: reignite growth by aggressively pursuing new customer acquisition. In essence, our approach emphasizes new customer acquisition's role in driving future growth.

By leveraging these insights, you'll chart a path to success. Join us as we bridge the gap between marketing and finance and propel your ecommerce brand forward!
