For most brands, monthly revenue targets are either:

Arbitrary — set by leadership because they want to grow a certain amount year-over-year (even if that’s impossible)

Delivered by a finance team that has no idea what’s even possible on the marketing side

In this video, CTC Director of Growth Strategy Luke Austin breaks down how to end that cycle and set monthly targets that align with both the marketing

and

financial realities of your business. You’ll learn how to:

Determine revenue targets based on real operating costs. How much money do you really need to make to stay afloat?

Set goals across acquisition channels
