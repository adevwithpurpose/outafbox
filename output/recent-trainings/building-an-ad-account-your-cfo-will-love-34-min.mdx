Welcome to another episode of Bridges, where we're bridging the gap between marketing and finance to help marketers secure a seat at the financial table! In today's episode, we're diving deep into the crucial task of building an ad account that your CFO will love. Your CFO might have never stepped into your ad account, but after today, they'll care deeply about it because we're guiding you on how to create an ad account that impacts cash flow, balances the sheets, and boosts the P&L simultaneously.

In this episode, we explore the fascinating intersection of merchandising, creative, and finance. A friend of CTC shared a unique perspective in our Twitter debate about who should own the forecast – marketing or finance. His take? Merchandising should take the lead as it sets revenue guidance, with marketing and finance playing supporting roles. Merchandising, he argues, is the key to forecasting success in a consumer-centric DTC business.

Today's focus is on transforming your ad account into a merchandising masterpiece that aligns creative efforts with financial and operational goals. We break down the concept of merchandising and introduce the three A's – Attention, Availability, and Allowance – as the pillars for building a successful ad account.

Attention is the first step. In a world flooded with content, capturing your audience's attention is the gateway to success. We analyze attention-grabbing strategies, emphasizing the importance of visual equity and color selection to stand out in a crowded digital space.

Availability is the next critical aspect. We introduce a practical inventory dashboard that empowers marketers to understand stock levels, sales velocity, and days of inventory remaining. The key is to align marketing efforts with inventory realities, avoiding stockouts and maximizing the marginal value of every unit.

Allowance is the final element. We challenge traditional ROAS-focused thinking, urging marketers to shift their mindset to maximize the marginal value of available units. By strategically managing high-demand, low-supply products and liquidating excess inventory, marketers can optimize the financial impact of their ad accounts.

Join us as we explore the dynamic world of merchandising in the context of building an ad account your CFO will love. Transform your approach, embrace the merchant mindset, and bridge the gap between creative, operations, and finance to drive holistic business success!

Don't miss out on valuable insights – hit that play button and embark on the journey to elevate your ad account game!
