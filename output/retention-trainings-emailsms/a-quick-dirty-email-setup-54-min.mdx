A Quick + Dirty Email Setup (54 min)

Email Marketing

In this training, we cover quick and dirty email setup, especially for DTC businesses that don't have much time/bandwidth to dedicate to email.

If you've ever.....

• Admired another brand's email content and thought "I just don't have time to do that"

• Experienced low engagement due to long gaps in between email sends

• Wished you could build out more flows and automations for customer journeys

this talk is for you.

We'll cover:

• Utilizing Klaviyo templates for flows & campaigns

• What to send when you don't know what to send

• How regular sending helps deliverability + engagement

• Foundational flows

everyone

should have in place
