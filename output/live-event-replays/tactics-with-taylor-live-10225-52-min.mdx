Tactics with Taylor Live 10/2/25 (52 min)

Live Event Replays

CTC CEO Taylor Holiday answer's members' questions on hiring, ad account security, Amazon strategy, and more.

Timestamps/Summary:

[00:00:00 – 00:05:35] Hiring & Training Challenges

Discussion on structuring agency roles when client size is small.

Struggles with finding “growth managers” who can think beyond paid media.

Debate between training smart generalists vs. paying more for experienced specialists.

Hidden costs of under-hiring and the trade-offs between salary levels and competence .

[00:11:17 – 00:14:15] Content Value & Performance Metrics

Evaluating whether content truly adds measurable value in ad accounts.

Dangers of ambiguous “brand value” arguments without clear metrics.

Push for tying organic/brand initiatives back to direct traffic, search, or revenue .

[00:14:53 – 00:23:10] AI Content & Risk Portfolio Analogy

Audience question on leveraging AI (e.g., Zora 2) for scaling content across platforms.

Taylor frames it as a risk/reward portfolio decision—treating marketing bets like investments.

Balancing wealth-creation style risks vs. wealth-preservation stability .

[00:21:45 – 00:24:10] Ad Account Shutdowns & Redundancy Risk

Meta ad account suspensions create major disruption.

Debate on whether to diversify pixels/ad accounts as redundancy.

Comparison to supply chain risk and earthquake insurance—hard to balance cost vs. probability .

[00:24:20 – 00:29:05] Product Launch Strategy (Suits & Blazers)

Framework for launching new products at higher price points.

Emphasis on breaking down revenue expectations: existing vs. new customers.

Execution tactics: email/SMS campaigns, influencer seeding, VIP outreach, phased discounting.

[00:39:56] Channel Diversification & Amazon Strategy

Supplements market competitiveness: customer acquisition costs driven by who can spend more.

Using Amazon defensively (visibility & trust), not necessarily for scalable growth .

[00:40:05 – 00:43:35] SEO, AI Traffic & Content Repurposing

Question on whether brands should lean into ChatGPT-driven traffic.

Caution against “white whale” stories of sudden SEO wins.

Taylor notes early returns are minuscule across 170+ CTC clients.

Content repurposing (ads into shorts/blogs) seen as low-value unless costs are negligible .

[00:44:40 – 00:46:25] Loopholes & Ad Policy Risks

Example of brands using subscription pricing in Google Shopping feeds.

Warning against “trick” tactics—short-term wins often lead to long-term penalties (loopholes close, accounts risk suspension) .

[00:46:26 – 00:47:40] In-house vs. Agency Content Ideation

Decision should hinge on “who’s the smartest person available,” regardless of whether they’re in-house, freelance, or at an agency .

[00:50:34 – 00:51:40] Ad Account Security & Wrap-Up

Risks of hackers exploiting high spending limits on ad accounts.

Best practices: restrict access, remove ex-employees/freelancers, set spend limits.
