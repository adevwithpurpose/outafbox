Media Buyers Jam 9/11/25 (55 min)

Live Event Replays

CTC Director of Paid Social Delaney Quinn answers members' questions on everything Meta Ad Buying.

Timestamps:

[00:00:00–00:06:20] Challenges with spend and AOV

Struggles with cost caps and low spend despite new bundle builder landing page.

Discussion on whether creative fatigue vs. cost control is the issue.

Suggestions: try one-day view attribution and mid-funnel add-to-cart campaigns.

[00:12:20–00:16:30] Campaign structure & underspending

Debate over combining different audience angles in a single ad set.

Frustrations with campaigns underspending despite historical success.

Guidance: analyze CPMs, creative fatigue, and funnel metrics (view → add-to-cart → checkout → purchase).

[00:24:50–00:27:00] CPMs and awareness campaigns

High CPMs across the board for video and static.

Should brands open awareness campaigns?

Advice: avoid pure awareness, focus on mid-funnel to build qualified traffic for Q4 prep.

[00:26:50–00:28:30] Avoiding creative fatigue

Refresh existing top-performing ad sets with new creatives instead of creating new structures.

Creative fatigue shows up first in upper/mid-funnel costs climbing.

[00:32:50–00:37:30] Audience overlap & account evaluation

Overlap of ~15% is acceptable; manage it with creative diversification.

New account manager asks how to evaluate performance.

Recommended: monitor creative performance, define audiences, use audience segment reporting in Ads Manager.

[00:38:57–00:41:30] Managing creative volume & ad structures

What to do with 50+ creatives weekly.

Guidance: structure by product categories/audiences instead of launching new ad sets each time.

Beauty/haircare: videos for engagement, statics often convert better.

Best to use a mix.

[00:41:40–00:44:30] Attribution and campaign structures

Meta ASC (Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns) changes explained.

Attribution: start with 7-day click; add 1-day view only if struggling with signal.

Reminder: adding view inflates reported performance.

[00:49:20–00:52:30] Q4 preparation and platform recommendations

Discussion of partnership ads and budget allocation.

Biggest tactical shift: lean-gen campaigns to grow email lists & retargeting pools before holiday surge.

Meta recommending adding “successful ads” into campaigns → usually can ignore unless new placement suggested.

[00:53:30–00:55:35] Diagnosing sales slowdowns

Distinguishing between poor offers, stockouts, competition, and weak creative signals.

Check CPMs, frequency, and funnel drop-offs (add-to-cart → checkout).

Stockouts often cause high CTR but low conversion.
