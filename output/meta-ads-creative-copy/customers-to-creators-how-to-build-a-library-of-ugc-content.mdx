Customers to Creators: How to Build a Library of UGC Content

Creative

Build an endless flow of UGC from real customers FOR FREE!

Here’s the flow that

@bambuearth

(natural skincare vertical)

uses to build an ongoing supply of authentic ad content:

23-33 days after purchase (depending on the SKU) the email below gets triggered.

Subject line:

We want to give you $50!

Any response to this email triggers a

@gorgiasio

macro that replies with this (see image below)

TLDR:

"Share a video of your skin care routine and we’ll send you a $50 gift card. Here’s a YouTube example for inspo!"

Users then respond and simply email the videos back! And *poof* you start getting videos you can use in ads!

Obviously, sometimes they need a little coaching to get things dialed in and useable in ads. Some of the Bambu Earth team's response is below:

Once you receive the videos, we recommend placing them in a central location (dropbox, Google Drive, etc) that you

can pull from whenever you need new creative.

generates
