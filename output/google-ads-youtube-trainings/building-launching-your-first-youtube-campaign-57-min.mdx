Building + Launching Your First YouTube Campaign (57 min)

YouTube Trainings

Before watching this training, you may be considering whether this is a channel worth exploring. Consider the following:

First, we would say that unless you feel as though you've maximized your opportunity on FB/IG, resist the temptation to put your limited time & resources into exploring multi-channel advertising efforts. If you're finding success on FB/IG and feel comfortable with the platform, it may be time to start branching out. But as DTC operators ourselves, we would focus my efforts on FB/IG first, them move to Google, and then maybe explore other channels like Youtube/Snap/Pinterest etc.

Second, if you're asking "What kinds of brands or verticals are worth testing on YT?" We wouldn't say that there are specific verticals or brands that do better on YT. Oftentimes, we do see higher AOV products that require more education do well on YT. That said, there can be opportunity for just about any brand/
