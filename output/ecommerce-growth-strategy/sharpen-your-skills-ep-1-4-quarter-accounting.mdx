Sharpen Your Skills, Ep. 1: 4 Quarter Accounting

Sharpen Your Skills Series

In this episode, Taylor tackles the 4 Quarter Accounting method—a simple but powerful way to interpret your Profit & Loss (P&L) statement for greater clarity and control. With just four key categories— Customer Acquisition Costs (CAC), Cost of Delivery, Operating Expenses (OPEX), and Profit, this approach offers a clear view of where your revenue is going and how to make more profitable decisions.

In This Episode:

- Understanding the 4-Quarter Accounting breakdown

- Real-life examples of how businesses use this model for profitability

- Tips on optimizing each quarter to maximize your bottom line
