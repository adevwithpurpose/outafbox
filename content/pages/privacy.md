      ## What We Collect

      We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you fill out a form, apply for a Growth Audit, or contact us via email.

      **Information includes:**
      - Name and email address
      - Company/brand information
      - Ad performance data (only if you grant access for an audit)

      ---

      ## How We Use Your Data

      We use your information to:
      - Provide our services and respond to your requests
      - Analyze your funnel and prepare audit reports
      - Send you relevant updates (only if you opt-in)

      **We do NOT sell your data. Ever.**

      ---

      ## Data Security

      We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data transmissions are encrypted via SSL/TLS.

      ---

      ## Third Parties

      We use trusted third-party services for:
      - Analytics (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)
      - Email (ConvertKit)
      - Hosting (Vercel, Cloudflare)

      These providers have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

      ---

      ## Your Rights

      You have the right to:
      - Access the data we hold about you
      - Request deletion of your data
      - Opt-out of marketing communications

      To exercise these rights, email us at **privacy@outafbox.com**.

      ---

      ## Changes

      We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our site.

      ---

      **Questions?** Contact us at privacy@outafbox.com.
